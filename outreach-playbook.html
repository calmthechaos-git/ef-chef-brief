The Core Principle

Speaker outreach IS attendee outreach. You don't need to figure out how to reach 5,000 people. You need 10-15 speakers who each have audiences of 10K-100K. When they each send one email and post twice, the attendees come to you.

Speakers confirm → Speakers promote → Attendees register → All-Access sells → Community launches

Part 1: Speaker Outreach

The Sequence

Days 1-2 Research the person. Read their recent posts. Listen to a podcast episode. Find something specific you genuinely connect with.
Day 3 Send the pitch. Use the template below. Personalize the first 1-2 sentences.
Day 7 Follow up #1. Short, gentle. "Just floating this back to the top."
Day 14 Follow up #2 / Final. "Last note from me. Thank you for the work you do."

4-Week Outreach Cadence

Week 1 Tier A — Highest Yes Rate Smaller platforms, lived experience, would benefit from exposure 15 pitches
Week 2 Tier B — Medium Likelihood Established professionals + Tier A follow-ups 13 new
Week 3 Tier C — Use Social Proof Bigger names. Include confirmed speakers as proof. 12 new
Week 4 Tier D — Dream Keynotes Long shots that change everything if they say yes 7 new

Daily commitment: 3-5 personalized emails per day. That's it.

Part 3: Attendee Outreach

Prerequisite: Do NOT promote the summit publicly until you have at least 3 confirmed speakers on the page. Otherwise it looks empty.

Phase 1

Warm Audience (Your Existing People)

  • Every Substack article includes a P.S. about the summit
  • Dedicated summit announcement post when registration opens
  • Speaker spotlight posts (one per confirmed speaker)
  • "Why I'm building this" personal story post
  • Summit announcements on Facebook
  • Behind-the-scenes content — "here's what I'm building and why"
  • Keep Happy Wins-day going — don't let the summit take over the vibe
Phase 2

Speaker Audiences (The Multiplier)

Make it stupidly easy for speakers to promote. Provide each one with:

  • 2-3 pre-written social posts (copy/paste ready)
  • 1 pre-written email for their list
  • Their custom affiliate link
  • Graphics: their headshot + summit branding, sized for IG/FB/email

The math: 10 speakers, avg 20K followers each = 200K reach. Even 1% click = 2,000 registrations.

Phase 3

Community Engagement (Free, Organic)

  • Reddit (r/ARFID, r/Autism_Parenting, r/adhdwomen) — answer questions, provide value
  • Facebook groups — join, contribute, mention summit when genuinely helpful
  • Podcast guesting — pitch yourself as a guest on ND parenting + eating shows
  • Substack cross-promo — Neurodivergent Rebel, Learn with Dr Emily, Rethinking Wellness
Phase 4

Paid Ads (LATER)

Only after organic registration proves the page converts.

  • Facebook/Instagram ads targeting parents of autistic children
  • Retargeting ads to website visitors who didn't register
  • Start at $10-20/day

Reddit Engagement Rules

Do

  • Answer questions with helpful, detailed responses
  • Share personal experience when relevant
  • Link to summit ONLY when someone asks for resources
  • Be a member for 2+ weeks before mentioning anything

Don't

  • Post "check out my summit!" threads
  • Drop links in every comment
  • Create fake accounts
  • Treat it as a marketing channel
